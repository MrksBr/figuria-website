Figuria

Privacy

Figuria does not collect or receive personal data and does not send gameplay data to the developer’s servers. The app does not use user accounts, remote analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app or cross-site tracking.

To make the daily puzzle work, Figuria stores limited gameplay information locally on your device, such as the current day’s puzzle state, selected difficulty, progress, tap history (used only for in-game functionality), panel state, and whether a reminder has been scheduled. This information is used only to restore your current game and app state when you reopen the app.

If you choose to enable reminders, Figuria may request notification permission in order to schedule a local notification for the next day’s puzzle. These notifications are created and delivered by iOS on your device and do not require sending data to the developer. Reminder settings are stored locally on your device.

Because Figuria does not collect or store personal data externally, there is no server-side user data to access, export, or delete. You can remove all locally stored app data at any time by deleting the app from your device.
