Figuria

Privacy

Figuria does not collect personal data and does not send gameplay data to the developer’s servers. The app does not use user accounts, remote analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app or cross-site tracking.

To make the daily puzzle work, Figuria stores limited gameplay information locally on your device, such as the current day’s puzzle state, selected difficulty, progress, tap history, panel state, and whether a reminder has been scheduled. This information is used only to restore your current game and app state when you reopen the app.

If you choose to enable reminders, Figuria may request notification permission in order to schedule a local notification for the next day’s puzzle. Reminder settings are stored locally on your device.

Figuria does not share user data with third parties. Because the app does not collect or transmit personal data, there is no server-side user data to access, export, or delete. If you remove the app from your device, its locally stored app data is removed with it.
